Contract
i
Before starting cooperation, you have to sign a main service agreement
between you (as a sole proprietor) and the company PROFITSOFT.
It will be sent to you for signing within 7 business days after the final review of your application.

Main part of the contract
The contract defines the subject of cooperation, the format of interaction, payment procedure, responsibilities of the parties, conditions for processing confidential information, and rights to the results of the work.

Appendix №1 — List of accesses
i
Appendix №1 is an integral part of the agreement and is not signed separately.
Defines which accounts, technical tools, and resources are provided to the contractor for the execution of work. It also contains rules regarding the safe use of these resources, restricted access, and obligations related to maintaining confidentiality.

Appendix №2 — Technical requirements
i
Appendix №2 is an integral part of the agreement and is not signed separately.
Describes technical requirements for the working environment, in particular: type of equipment (personal or corporate), data protection requirements (antivirus, encryption, access control), software usage conditions, and the possibility of technical administration by the company.
